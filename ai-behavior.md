# AI Behavior

Smart AI Approvals uses AI for decision guidance only.

---

## What AI Does

- summarizes deal & related data
- highlights risks
- explains recommendation

---

## What AI Does NOT Do

- approve deals
- enforce policy
- replace human judgment

---

## Bring Your Own LLM (BYOM)

Smart AI Approvals does not require a bundled model subscription.

Customers choose which LLM provider to use and configure their own connection and credentials.

This gives teams flexibility over:
- provider preference
- cost management
- security posture

---

## Data Usage

Only structured deal data is sent.

Admins control:

- field mappings
- data exposure

---

## Output Quality

Depends on:

- data quality
- policy configuration
- mapping accuracy
