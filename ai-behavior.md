# AI Behavior

Smart AI Approvals uses AI for decision support only.

---

## What AI Does

- summarizes deal data
- highlights risks
- explains recommendation

---

## What AI Does NOT Do

- approve deals
- enforce policy
- replace human judgment

---

## Data Usage

Only structured deal data is sent.

Admins control:

- field mappings
- data exposure

---

## Output Quality

Depends on:

- data quality
- policy configuration
- mapping accuracy
