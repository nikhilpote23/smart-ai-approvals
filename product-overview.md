# Product Overview

Smart AI Approvals is a Salesforce-native approval intelligence layer. It is designed to improve approval efficiency and decision quality for revenue-related workflows. The package generates structured approval summaries, evaluates configurable approval policies, compares current deals with historical deals, and records approval outcomes with a full audit trail.

---

## Problem

Approvers must navigate:

- Opportunity
- Quote
- Pricing
- Contract terms

This causes:

- slow approvals
- inconsistent decisions
- margin leakage

---

## Solution

A single decision interface:

- AI Deal Brief
- Policy Evaluation
- Recommendation

---

## Core Capabilities

- Approval Request lifecycle
- AI Deal Brief generation
- Policy-based evaluation
- Recommendation reasoning
- Ask AI follow-up

---

## Supported Objects

- Salesforce CPQ Quote
- Opportunity

---

## Principle

AI recommends, Humans make decisions.
