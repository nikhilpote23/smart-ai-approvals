# Quickstart (10 Minutes)

Get from install → first approval.

---

## Step 1 — Install

Deploy Smart AI Approvals to your Salesforce org.

---

## Step 2 — Assign Permissions

Assign:

- Approval_Admin
- Approval_Approver
- Approval_Submitter

---

## Step 3 — Configure AI

Set up Named Credential for your AI provider.

---

## Step 4 — Create First Approval

1. Open SmartAIApprovals app
2. Create Approval Request
3. Submit

---

## Step 5 — Review Brief

Open:

👉 AI Deal Brief tab

You should see:

- summary
- recommendation
- analysis

---

## Done

Your approval workflow is live.
