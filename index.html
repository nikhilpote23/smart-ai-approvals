How it works

A blog-style walkthrough of the product flow.

This is the exact story your buyer should understand within the first minute.

1

A submitter creates an approval request

The request links the opportunity, quote, exception notes, and business context that need review.

2

The system evaluates policy thresholds

Discount, payment terms, margin, and related rule inputs are checked against approval policy logic.

3

AI generates a decision brief

The product summarizes the deal into a format an approver can read in seconds, not minutes.

4

Approvers review benchmark context and recommendation

Similar deals, risk signals, and recommendation reasoning make the decision easier and more consistent.

5

Decision gets recorded with clarity

Approvers act with better context, faster turnaround, and stronger alignment to policy.
